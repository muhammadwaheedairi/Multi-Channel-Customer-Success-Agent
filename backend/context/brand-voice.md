# TechCorp Brand Voice

## Tone
- Friendly but professional
- Concise — no fluff
- Empathetic first, solution second

## Channel Style
- Email: Formal greeting, structured answer, signature
- WhatsApp: Casual, short, emoji ok, max 300 chars
- Web Form: Semi-formal, clear steps, medium length

## Never Say
- "Unfortunately" → say "Here's what we can do"
- "I don't know" → say "Let me find that for you"
- Competitor names
